# Fallback Sources

Use this reference when the official customs platform is unavailable or the user asks for cross-validation.

## Mysteel / Longzhong

Mysteel and Longzhong sometimes republish customs-derived chemical export data.

Use them as fallback or cross-validation, not as the official source.

Known behavior:

- Free article previews can include headline volume, average price, MoM/YoY, and top destination.
- Detailed province tables are commonly paywalled.
- Publishing cadence is often around the 20th-23rd of the month for prior-month data.

## WF6 Cross-Validation Note

As of the prior observed workflow, Mysteel free-tier headline data matched official customs for WF6 April/May 2026 headline volume and average price. Treat that as a source-quality note, not a permanent guarantee.

When using fallback data, state:

- source name
- whether it is official or secondary
- what exact metric is visible free-tier vs paywalled
- whether it was cross-validated against customs for the requested period

## Do Not

- Do not present fallback data as official customs data.
- Do not infer province/company rows from paywalled snippets unless rows are visible.
- Do not mix Mysteel article dates with customs data months; report both.
